Boiled Fried Poached Scrambled Plain Omelette

Fry it up!

Ingredients

Eggs
Oil
Salt & Pepper

Method:

1. Crack the eggs into a pan of shallow hot oil.
2. Cook slowly, basting occasionally.
3. Drain off excess fat and serve
4. Serve on a suitable dish
5. Season to taste
