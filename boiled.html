Boiling the Egg

Ingredients

Eggs (Room Temperature)
Salt
Water

Method:

1. Fill a suitable pan with enough water to cover the eggs, add salt to taste.
2. Bring up to the boil and place the eggs in, being careful not to crack them.
3. For soft boiled, approximate time is 3-4 mins (depending on size) after the water is brought back to the boil.
4. For hard-boilded, 12 mins after the water is brought back to the boil.

Note:

• Store hard-boilded eggs in the fridge, remove shell, cover in clean water and cling-film for up to 7 days.
• It is best to use eggs that are at room temperature, this will help with accurate timing.

Equipment List:

2 ltr Saucepan
Perforated Spoon
Egg Cup
Side Plate
